OverviewPTC India Ltd. Headquartered in New Delhi and employing around 115 people, the company operates primarily as a zero-asset power trading entity. For the trailing twelve months as of December 31, 2025, PTC reported a revenue of $1.81 billion and a net income of $97.717 million. A key subsidiary, PTC India Financial Services (PFS), in which PTC holds a 64.99% stake, operates as an Infrastructure Finance Company. PTC recently divested its entire stake in its former subsidiary, PTC Energy Limited, which had a renewable portfolio of 288.8 MW.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesPTC India's operations are primarily divided into two main segments: the Power segment and the Financing Business segment. The Financing Business segment, operated through its subsidiary PTC India Financial Services, extends debt and equity to power projects across the value chain, from generation to distribution.
