A signi ficant portion of Olectra's business is conducted through Evey Trans Private Limited, an entity with which it has related party transactions. For instance, an order for 1,085 buses, valued at approximately ₹1,800 crores, was awarded to Evey Trans, which will procure the buses from Olectra, with Olectra also handling maintenance for a 12-year period. While the se transactions are stated to be at arm's length, their scale and nature necessitate robust oversight from the Audit Committee to ensure fairness and transparency.
5. 4 Corporate GovernanceThe company has established several board committees, including an Audit Committee, a Nomination & Remuneration Committee, a Stakeholders Relationship Committee, a Corporate Social Responsibility Committee, and a Risk Management Committee, to oversee various aspects of its governance. According to its 2024 Sustainability Report, Olectra has achieved 33.33% female representation in its leadership and provided human rights training to 100% of its workforce, demonstrating a commitment to certain ESG principles. The compa ny's governance framework also includes a policy for considering and approving related party transactions, which was updated in August 2025, to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations. However, the company's high working capital intensity, with Net Working Capital to Operating Income (NWC/OI) at 38% as of March 31, 2024, and its reliance on its parent company, MEIL, for financial support in the past, are areas that require continued scrutiny from a corporate governance perspective.
