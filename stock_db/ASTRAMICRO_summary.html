OverviewAstra Microwave Products Ltd. (AMPL), founded in 1991, is a prominent Indian company engaged in the design, development, and manufacturing of radio frequency (RF) and microwave super components, sub-systems, and systems. The company primarily serves strategic sectors including defense, space, and meteorology, playing a crucial role in India's 'Make in India' initiative. For the fiscal year 2024-25, the company reported consolidated revenue of ₹1,051 crore, demonstrating its significant operational scale. As of 2025, AMPL has been a key contributor to major national programs, having developed critical components for 35 different radar systems, 25 satellites, and 15 strategic defense programs, showcasing its deep integration into the nation's strategic infrastructure.
OverviewHeadquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana, Astra Microwave Products operates multiple specialized production facilities, including Units 1, 2, 3, 4, and 7, to support its manufacturing activities. The company's commitment to innovation is underscored by its dedicated R&D center located at the Bangalore Aerospace Park in Karnataka. Its technological prowess is highlighted by its capability to develop advanced Gallium Nitride (GaN) and Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) MMIC products, a skill possessed by few private firms in India. For the twelve months ending December 2025, the company's revenue peaked at ₹10.825 billion, a significant increase from its five-year low of ₹6.464 billion in March 2021, reflecting substantial growth in its operations.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesAstra Microwave's revenue is primarily driven by its engagement in high-technology sectors, with a significant focus on defense applications. The revenue distribution as of the second quarter of fiscal year 2026 illustrates this concentration: the Defense sector accounted for 77.4% of the total, followed by Exports (including Deemed Exports) at 14.2%, the Meteorological sector at 5.8%, and the Space sector at 2.0%, with other activities contributing the remaining 0.6%. The company's core offerings include advanced radar electronics, electronic warfare (EW) systems, and strategic telemetry equipment. Within its EW segment, the company designs and develops wide-band super-components operating in critical frequency ranges from 0.5 to 18 GHz and from 18 to 40 GHz.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesThe company's largest revenue-generating segment is Radar Electronics, where it provides critical components and sub-systems for a wide array of ground, naval, and airborne radar applications. Its extensive product portfolio in this area includes Active Antenna Array Units, TR Modules across multiple frequency bands (V/UHF, L, S, C, X, Ku, and Ka), Solid State Power Amplifiers, and Receiver Exciters up to the Ka-Band. Since 2004, Astra Microwave has also been a key partner to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), contributing to every major Indian satellite program since 2007 by providing essential flight model and ground-based electronic systems, showcasing its long-standing expertise and reliability in the space domain.
