OverviewRailTel Corporation of India Ltd. (RailTel) is a "Navratna" Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) under the Ministry of Railways, established in September 2000 to modernize the Indian Railways' telecommunications system. It is one of India's largest neutral telecom infrastructure providers, with an extensive optical fiber network of over 63,000 route kilometers laid along railway tracks, covering more than 7,000 railway stations. The company also has a citywide access network of over 21,000 kilometers and more than 11,000 Points of Presence (PoPs). R ailTel is a debt-free company, holding Unified Licenses for NLD, ISP, and ILD services. As of 2022, the company had 730 employees. T he Government of India holds a 72.84% stake in the company. R ailTel achieved Navratna status in the 2024-2025 fiscal year.

Capacity DetailsRailTel Corporation of India commands an extensive and robust telecom infrastructure.
