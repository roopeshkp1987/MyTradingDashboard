OverviewFinolex Cables Ltd. (FCL) is India's largest manufacturer of electrical and communication cables, with a history spanning over six decades since its founding in 1958. The company is headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, and is publicly listed on both the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and the National Stock Exchange (NSE). FCL operates five modern, state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities located in Pimpri (Pune), Urse (Pune), Goa (Verna and Ponda), and Roorkee (Uttarakhand), all of which are ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified. The company employs approximately 1,600 full-time individuals and has a vast distribution network comprising 26 depots, around 5,000 channel partners, and over 215,000 retailers across 28 states and 8 Union Territories, ensuring extensive market penetration.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesFinolex Cables' operations are diversified across four primary segments: Electrical Cables, Communication Cables, Copper Rods, and Others, which includes a growing portfolio of Fast-Moving Electrical Goods (FMEG). In the fiscal year 2024-25, the Electrical Wires and Cables segment was the largest revenue contributor, accounting for 84% of the total revenue with ₹4,468 crore. T he Communication Cables segment followed, contributing 10% or ₹532 crore to the total revenue. The Copper Rods segment and Other Products (FMEG) made up the remaining portion, with 1% (₹56 crore) and 5% (₹256 crore) respectively. The com pany's total revenue for this period reached ₹5,319 crore, reflecting a 6% year-over-year growth. This stra tegic diversification into the FMEG sector, which includes products like LED lighting, fans, switches, and water heaters, is part of a broader strategy to transition into a comprehensive electrical products provider.
Market Share & Customer BaseFinolex Cables holds a commanding position in the Indian wire and cable industry, with an estimated market share of 23.9% in the organized sector as of March 2025, solidifying its status as a top-three player in the nation's manufacturing landscape for these products. The compa ny's primary competitors in this space include Polycab India Ltd., Havells India Ltd., KEI Industries Ltd., and RR Kabel Ltd. Finolex Cables caters to a broad customer base that spans both retail and institutional markets across critical sectors such as construction, automotive, telecommunications, and solar energy. The compa ny's extensive distribution network, which includes over 400 cities and more than 3,300 dealers, enables it to effectively serve its diverse clientele throughout India and in export markets like the United States and the United Arab Emirates.
Capacity DetailsFinolex Cables is undertaking a significant capacity expansion and modernization initiative, with a planned capital expenditure of nearly ₹500 crore between fiscal years 2024 and 2026. This invest ment is aimed at enhancing its manufacturing capabilities across its five production facilities in Pimpri, Urse, Goa, and Roorkee. In a previous expansion phase, the company invested approximately Rs 400 million to increase the capacity of its compounding plant and various copper cable production lines at its Urse facility. The company also established a captive 5 MW Solar Power Plant at its Urse manufacturing site, demonstrating a commitment to sustainable energy practices. Finolex's i ntegrated manufacturing model, which includes the in-house production of its own copper rods and PVC compounds, ensures stringent quality control throughout the supply chain.
