Regulatory EnvironmentThe regulatory framework for Registrar and Transfer Agents (RTAs) in India is primarily governed by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), with the foundational rules laid out in the SEBI (Registrars to an Issue and Share Transfer Agents) Regulations, 1993. These changes aim to enhance transparency and simplify the language of the regulations.
