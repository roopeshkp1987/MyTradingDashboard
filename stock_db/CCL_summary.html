It employs approximately 1,965 people, though headcount saw a 3.1% decline in 2025 from 1,931 employees in 2024. Headquartered in Hyderabad, India, CCL operates four key state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities located in India (Duggirala and Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh), Vietnam, and Switzerland, providing strategic access to raw materials and key consumer markets across Europe and Asia. This extensive infrastructure supports the production of over 900 distinct coffee blends.
