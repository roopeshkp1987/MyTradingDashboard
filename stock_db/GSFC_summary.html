T he company operates its primary manufacturing facilities at Fertilizernagar (Vadodara) and Sikka. A s of August 31, 2025, the company employed approximately 2,440 people. F or the fiscal year 2025-26, GSFC reported a significant 15% rise in operating revenue to Rs. 10,827 crore and a 14% increase in Profit After Tax to Rs. 652 crore, driven by its highest fertilizer production in five years, which reached 17.59 lakh metric tonnes. T he Government of Gujarat, through Gujarat State Investments Limited (GSIL), is the promoter and held a 37.84% equity stake in the company as of December 31, 2025.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesGSFC operates through two primary segments: Fertilizer Products and Industrial Products. For fiscal year 2025, the fertilizer segment was the dominant contributor, accounting for approximately 77% of the company's total operating income and 93% of its profitability. In fiscal year 2025-26, fertilizer sales surged by 17% (Rs. 1,196 crore), with volumes increasing by 12% to 22.31 lakh metric tonnes. Key fertilizer products include Urea, Ammonium Sulphate (AS), Di-ammonium Phosphate (DAP), and Ammonium Phosphate Sulphate (APS). The company's diverse product portfolio features over 24 brands spanning fertilizers, petrochemicals, chemicals, industrial gases, plastics, and fibres, catering to a wide range of market needs from agricultural to industrial consumers.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesThe Industrial Products segment complements the fertilizer business, contributing the remaining 23% to revenue and 7% to profitability in FY25. This division manufactures a range of high-value chemicals including Caprolactam, Nylon-6, Melamine, and Methanol. In fi scal year 2025-26, this segment demonstrated a strong turnaround, with sales rising 9% and EBIT growing significantly from Rs. 56 crore to Rs. 200 crore, marking its highest yearly profitability in four years. For t he nine months ending December 31, 2025, the industrial products segment sales reached Rs. 596 crore, and EBIT turned positive to Rs. 9 crores, driven by higher Melamine exports and traded Ammonia sales.
Market Share & Customer BaseGSFC holds a dominant market position in India for several of its key industrial products, reinforcing its diversified business model. The com pany is recognized as the sole domestic manufacturer of Melamine and the largest producer of Caprolactam and Nylon-6 in the country.
