OverviewNMDC Steel Limited (NSL), incorporated on January 2, 2015, is a central public sector enterprise operating under India's Ministry of Steel. The company runs a modern, 3 million tonnes per annum (MTPA) integrated steel plant in Nagarnar, Chhattisgarh, which was demerged from NMDC Limited. This greenfield project, strategically located near the Bailadila iron ore mines, utilizes a Blast Furnace/Basic Oxygen Furnace (BF/BOF) technological route to produce high-grade steel. The President of India holds a majority stake of approximately 60.79% in the company, which is publicly listed on both the BSE and NSE exchanges under the ticker NSLNISP. For the fiscal year 2024-25, the company reported revenue from operations amounting to ₹8,503.05 crore, a significant figure for its initial years of full-scale operation.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesNMDC Steel Limited's core business is centered on the manufacturing and sale of a variety of high-grade steel products. The primary offerings from its Nagarnar plant include Hot Rolled (HR) coils, HR sheets, and plates. These products cater to a wide array of industrial sectors, including infrastructure, automotive, energy, oil and gas, shipbuilding, and heavy engineering. In the fiscal year 2024-25, the sale of HR Coils was the principal revenue driver, generating ₹6,528.30 crore. Other sales, which encompass products like pig iron and various by-products, contributed an additional ₹1,974.75 crore to the total revenue from operations of ₹8,503.05 crore, demonstrating a focused yet diversifying product portfolio.
