OverviewGarden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers Ltd. (GRSE), incorporated in 1934 and taken over by the Government of India in 1960, is a key defence Public Sector Undertaking where the government maintains a majority 74.50% ownership stake. The company operates under the Ministry of Defence, playing a crucial role in India's maritime security. For the fiscal year 2024-25, GRSE's value of production was ₹5,071 crore, leading to a Profit After Tax (PAT) of ₹527 crore and a net worth of ₹2,079 crore. Headquartered in Kolkata, GRSE has grown from a small repair yard into a major engineering enterprise and was the first shipyard in India to export a warship, the CGS Barracuda to Mauritius, showcasing its significant capabilities.
Business Segments & Products/ServicesThe shipbuilding segment is the primary driver of GRSE's revenue, contributing 89% of the company's total income. This division focuses on the construction of a wide variety of defense vessels, primarily for the Indian Navy and the Indian Coast Guard. Its diverse product portfolio includes complex warships such as frigates, missile corvettes, anti-submarine warfare corvettes, survey vessels, and offshore patrol vessels. Beyond its core defense shipbuilding, GRSE also produces other engineering products and has previously supplied a range of items like pontoons, barges, fishing trawlers, and dredgers. The company's main manufacturing operations are centered in Kolkata, spread across 3 separate shipbuilding facilities to support its production demands.
Market Share & Customer BaseGRSE is a leading shipyard for India's defence sector, having built and delivered 108 warships for the Indian Navy and Indian Coast Guard over the last 63 years as of March 2024. The company holds the distinction of being the first shipyard in the country to deliver 100 warships, a milestone achieved with the delivery of LCU L-56 on March 30, 2019. This track record establishes its critical role as a primary supplier for the nation's maritime forces. In addition to its domestic focus, GRSE builds commercial vessels such as tankers and bulk carriers and serves international clients through exports. The company's operations are supported by a workforce of 1,645 employees as of March 2024, who are engaged in ship design, building, and repair services.
Capacity DetailsGarden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers Ltd. maintains a comprehensive infrastructure network with shipbuilding facilities located in Kolkata and a dedicated diesel engine plant in Ranchi. The company's production capacity is highlighted by a dry dock that can accommodate ships up to 26,000 tonnes deadweight (DWT), enabling the construction and repair of large vessels. For hull construction, the facilities include a main building berth and two slipways. The fitting-out process is supported by a covered, all-weather, non-tidal wet basin for smaller ships, as well as a larger fitting-out complex equipped with three berths alongside. Furthermore, GRSE operates two river jetties capable of berthing smaller vessels up to 60 metres in length.
