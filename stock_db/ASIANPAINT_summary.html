Regulatory EnvironmentThe regulatory landscape for the Indian paint industry is heavily focused on environmental, health, and safety (EHS) compliance, with specific regulations governing manufacturing processes and product composition. A key regulation is the EIA Notification of 2006, under which integrated paint industries involved in manufacturing ingredients like resins are categorized under schedule 5(h) and require prior Environmental Clearance (EC).
