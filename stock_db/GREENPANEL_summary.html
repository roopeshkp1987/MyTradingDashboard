The implement ation timeline is staggered, with large companies given 6 months, medium-sized companies 9 months, and small and micro-industries 12 months to comply. Additionally, the government is considering amending guidelines for wood-based industries, which would involve the formation of state-level committees and wood councils to govern aspects like the pricing of local farm wood.
