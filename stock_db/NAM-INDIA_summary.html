Regulatory RiskThe company faces significant regulatory scrutiny from the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), which can result in financial penalties and reputational damage. In August 2024, SEBI imposed a total penalty of Rs 3 lakh on Nippon Life India Asset Management Ltd and its associated trustee for violations concerning the Total Expense Ratio (TER). The asset management company was fined Rs 2 lakh, and the trustee was fined Rs 1 lakh, with a directive to pay the amount within 45 days of the order. The penalty stemmed from findings that the fund house did not adhere to a SEBI circular dated October 22, 2018, which mandates that all scheme-related expenses must be paid from the scheme itself. An examination revealed that in the case of 5 specific ETFs, the company absorbed excess expenses in its own books rather than charging them to the schemes, leading to regulatory action.
