Business RiskDLF faces notable business risks stemming from its high geographic concentration and considerable financial leverage compared to its peers. A significant portion of its operations and assets are focused in one area, with approximately 60% of its extensive land bank located within the National Capital Region (NCR), making the company exceptionally vulnerable to localized economic downturns, regulatory changes, or demand shifts in this single market. Compounding this issue is the loss of 4 experienced independent directors over the past 3 years and a complex group structure comprising over 100 subsidiaries, which requires robust oversight that could be challenged by the existing board composition.
